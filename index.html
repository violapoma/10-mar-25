Focaccia with yummy Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetixer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become you new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Souce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or ina stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in a bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
